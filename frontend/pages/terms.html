4. AI-Powered Moderation

Bixcart uses a two-layer AI moderation system to keep the platform safe:

  • Layer 1 (Keyword Filter): Instantly scans all messages and listings against our prohibited keyword list. Catches ~90% of violations in real-time.
  • Layer 2 (Gemini AI): Reviews ambiguous cases using advanced language models. Rate-limited to ensure fair usage.
  • Sweep Job: Every 26 hours, AI re-scans all active listings and recent messages to catch anything missed initially.

If your content is flagged:

  • You will receive a push notification explaining the violation
  • Flagged listings are hidden from the marketplace
  • Flagged conversations are marked and both parties are notified
  • Repeated violations may lead to account suspension
⚠️ Appeal Process If you believe your content was incorrectly flagged, contact moderation@bixcart.com with the listing/message ID and explanation. Human review will be completed within 48 hours.

  • Listings expire after 60 days of inactivity; you may renew them

Transactions & Payments

  • Bixcart facilitates connections between buyers and sellers — we are not a party to the transaction
  • We recommend in-person cash transactions or our escrow payment system (when available)
  • Bixcart is not responsible for disputes between users; we provide dispute resolution assistance
  • Never share sensitive financial information (passwords, PINs, OTPs)
